Common workplace dental injuries we assist with

If any of these happened at work, call or SMS and we’ll help you with next steps and paperwork.

Chipped or broken tooth

Pain, sharp edges, exposed dentine, or cracked enamel after impact.

Knocked or loose tooth

Tooth moved/loosened, or completely knocked out — urgent care matters.

Jaw / facial impact

Jaw pain, bite changes, swelling, or facial trauma after an incident.

Soft tissue injury

Cuts to lips/gums, bleeding, or oral trauma from workplace incidents.

Dental pain after incident

Delayed pain can still be injury-related — document and get assessed.

FAQ

Is “$0 down” always available?

“$0 down” may apply where eligible and is subject to insurer determination/approval.

What information should I have ready?

Incident date/time, workplace location, what happened, any photos if safe, and employer/insurer details if you have them.

What if I have heavy bleeding or severe trauma?

For heavy bleeding, severe pain, facial trauma or emergencies, call 000 immediately.
