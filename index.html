Common work-related dental injuries

Workplace incidents can cause dental trauma that needs assessment, pain relief, stabilisation and documentation.

Chipped or broken teeth

From workplace impact, falls, tools or machinery incidents.

Knocked, loose or displaced teeth

Urgent review is often needed to stabilise and plan treatment.

Jaw, facial or mouth trauma

Including jaw impact, facial injury, lip/gum trauma and bite issues.

Dental pain after an incident

Toothache, sensitivity, swelling or pain when chewing following trauma.

Soft tissue injuries

Gums, lips and oral tissue injuries that can require ongoing care.
